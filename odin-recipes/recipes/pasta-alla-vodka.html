Pasta alla Vodka

How to make pasta alla vodka

This pasta a la vodka is an easy recipe to throw together for a quick dinner any night of the week — the sauce can be made while you cook the pasta. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese to serve. Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes
  • ½ cup chopped fresh basil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup vodka
  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 1 pint heavy cream

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook garlic in olive oil until tender, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in tomatoes, breaking up a bit with a fork. Stir in basil, salt, and pepper and simmer 15 minutes. Stir in vodka and cook 15 minutes more.
  2. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. Stir cream into sauce and cook 10 minutes more. Toss with hot pasta.
